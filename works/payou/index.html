Payou is a Peer-to-Peer (P2P) mobile payment App with social network component in Hong Kong. It provides a simple, convenient and most fun way for friends to settle debts and pay money to each other.

Basically, users can make payment to their friends through Payou, with a payment descriptor and/or a photo attached. They can choose to make it public on the newsfeed, and share with everyone within the social network.
